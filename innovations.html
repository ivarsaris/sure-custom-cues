Custom cues

Shafts

All shafts are made in house from the best quality Canadian maple.

We provide shafts with four different optional taper versions:

  1. Compound (stiff)
  2. Parabolic (medium flexible)
  3. Pro (flexible)
  4. Super pro (extra flexible)

We have three different construction options available:

Full maple

Full maple shafts give an original wooden feel.

Full maple LD

Our low deflection shafts are made with a construction developed over many hours of research.

Sure carbon pro

Our hybrid maple carbon shaft is our most high-tech shaft. The shaft has a full carbon core. It plays with the consistency of a carbon shaft with the feeling of wood.

Extensions

For those hard to reach shots. An extension makes it possible to reach any shot while not compromising your stroke. There's a solution possible for almost every cue, so contact us to see what we can do for you. Most extensions are made from a solid black synthetic material or carbon fibre. Carbon fibre extensions are only available with a diameter of 30 or 32 mm.

Four piece break/jump cue

The four piece break/jump cue is our interpretation of how both a break cue and a jump cue should be. It combines a break cue, with three shaft options, and two different length jump cues. All in only one cue. As it is a series, we make them with the same handle every time. There are two options for the handle:

  • Purpleheart; medium light and hard
  • Ipe; heavy and strong

This cue safes space in your case and gives you the opportunity to play most shots in difficult situations.

SureRESO handle

The Reso, as it stands for resonation, was developed to provide the best feel for three-piece constructed cues in terms of hit and touch. When developing we wanted to replicate the feeling of a full spliced cue as closely as possible. Important for us was not to change the balance to the front or back. We believe a naturally balanced cue is the way to go.

Wraps

Like almost everything we offer, we also make wraps on our own. We work with specialized companies that provide us with the best raw materials possible. As we've done this for many years, we also sell wraps to other cue making colleagues in the business. We understand that a quality product starts with a quality material. Therefore, we like to be able to control this.

Dealer inquiries are welcome!

SPC Break/jump tips

SPC classic
  • Stability: The fiber reinforced material gives the tip an incomparable hardness and durability which results in an ultra long life span.
  • Power: The hardness of the tip ensures maximum power to your breaks with no energy lost in elasticity.
  • Control: The premium benefit of the tip is the unique rough-top surface which delivers and outstanding grip and maximum control on every shot. With no other tip one can make this many controlled jumps and breaks without rechalking.
SPC elastic
